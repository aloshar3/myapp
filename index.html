About the Project

Welcome to the Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) website! CERIT provides valuable educational resources related to designing and developing websites for undergraduate and graduate students. This website provides a comprehensive learning resources for JavaScript, HTML, CSS, PHP, web hosting and cloud computing. You are more than welcome to use these resources for your academic advancement in information technology (IT).
